Welcome to War Worlds, a new 4X MMO for Android, in the spirit of Master of Orion!

Before you begin, you might want to familiarize yourself with an overview of the game. Otherwise, feel free to jump right in!

The first thing you’ll be asked to do is set up your Empire. We’ll need to authenticate you with a Google Account (see our Privacy Policy if you have any privacy concerns), and then a couple of basic questions about who you want to be in the game is all that is required.

Click “Start” to get started!
