Welcome to War Worlds, a new 4X MMO for Android, in the spirit of Master of Orion!

If you are new to this kind of game, you might want to become familiar with the idea of 4X gaming, and MMO gaming as well.

For now, let's just say this is a game of galactic space war, played online by hundreds of players at a time.

The first thing you'll be asked to do is set up your Empire. We'll need to authenticate you with a Google Account, and then a couple of basic questions about who you want to be in the game, and that is all that is required.
